🎓 Student Transport Grant

Event-driven trigger from FHIR birth records. Auto-links to guardian household. Support queued for approval or auto-action.

Monthly allocation for households with poverty index < 0.4. Prioritizes elderly, disabled, and large-dependency families.

AI-guided budget targeting. CSR managers ask in Arabic → system ranks segments by need, geography, and unmet coverage. Mandatory HITL approval.
